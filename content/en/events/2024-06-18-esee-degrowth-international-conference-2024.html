ESEE-Degrowth International Conference 2024 will be held in Pontevedra in June of next year, and will jointly host the 10th International Degrowth Conference and the 15th Conference of the European Society for Ecological Economics.

The event is organized by the ]Post-Growth Innovation Lab group of the University of Vigo, in collaboration with the international degrowth movement, the Research and Degrowth Association, and the European Society for Ecological Economics. In addition to the academic activities of the conference, numerous citizen-oriented initiatives related to degrowth and ecological economics will be held in different venues in the city.

The website is already available and will provide updated information on this important event, which is expected to host more than 800 attendees, including academics and the general public, and will count on the best researchers in the field. Visit https://esee-degrowth2024.uvigo.gal/ to make sure you don’t miss anything.
